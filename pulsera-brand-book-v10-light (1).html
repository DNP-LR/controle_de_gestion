01 Brand Foundation

Who We Are

PULSERA is a legal and financial advisory firm defined by precision, integrity, and a commitment to enduring client relationships. Our identity reflects the pulse of modern business — measured, confident, and always forward-looking.

Mission
"To provide clarity and confidence at every critical crossroads of legal and financial decision-making."
Vision
"To be the most trusted advisory partner for organizations navigating complex regulatory and financial landscapes."
Positioning
"Where legal rigor meets financial intelligence — a singular, integrated advisory practice."
Promise
"Every engagement is handled with absolute discretion, methodical precision, and unwavering professional ethics."
Core Values
Integrity
Our work is grounded in honesty. We say what we mean, deliver what we promise, and act transparently at all times.
Precision
In law and finance, details are everything. We are methodical, thorough, and leave nothing to chance.
Discretion
Client confidentiality is sacred. Every engagement is handled with absolute professionalism and privacy.
Resilience
We help clients navigate uncertainty. The pulse doesn't stop — and neither does PULSERA.
Brand Personality
Tone of Voice
Measured & Authoritative
PULSERA speaks with confidence, never arrogance. Sentences are precise and deliberate. We avoid jargon unless it serves the client, and we never sensationalize.
Archetype
The Sage
PULSERA positions as the wise counsel — deeply knowledgeable, calm under pressure, trusted with sensitive matters. Clients come to us not for answers, but for wisdom.
Character
Composed, Never Cold
Professional warmth distinguishes PULSERA. We are structured but never rigid, formal but never distant. Every interaction feels considered and human.

05 Iconography

Icon System

PULSERA uses a custom icon set built on a 24×24px grid with 1.5px stroke weight. Icons are geometric, precise, and never merely decorative. They communicate function clearly and align with the brand's professional tone.

Icon Style Rules
Grid & Construction
All icons live on a 24×24px grid with 2px padding. Stroke width is consistently 1.5px. Use round line caps and joins for a refined, friendly-professional look.
Color Application
Icons default to White on dark surfaces. Gold accent is reserved for key action icons. Steel Blue is used for secondary functional states.
Sizing Scale
Approved sizes: 16px (inline), 20px (compact UI), 24px (standard), 32px (featured), 48px (display/illustration).

Margins & Grid
All documents follow a 12-column grid with 15mm outer margins. Interior bleed areas use 5mm. Content must never touch the bleed zone. Body text sits in columns 2–11, allowing column 1 and 12 as breathing room.
File Formats for Print
Print-ready: PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4
Vector logo: .ai, .eps, .svg
Raster logo: .png (transparent)
Color profile: ISO Coated v2
Gold Foil & Spot UV — All premium print collateral should use PMS 7508 C gold for foil stamping on the logo mark and key accent elements. Spot UV is approved for the logo icon only on business card backs and folder covers. Overuse of special finishes diminishes their prestige value.
